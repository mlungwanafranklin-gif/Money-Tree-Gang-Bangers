Our Story

From the
Garage Up

🌳
EST
2018

It started in 2018 in a rented garage in Tembisa, Johannesburg — six childhood friends who had nothing but a laptop, a cheap microphone, and an unshakeable belief that their voices mattered. Money Tree Gang Bangers was born not from ambition, but from necessity.

Their debut EP Planting Seeds circulated on WhatsApp before it ever hit streaming platforms. By 2019 they were headlining local shows; by 2020 their lockdown album Green Season had broken through continental boundaries, racking up millions of plays from Nigeria to Sweden.

The 2022 album Money Talks was a turning point — three SAMA nominations, a sold-out Johannesburg arena, and collaborations with artists from across Africa and the diaspora. MTGB was no longer just a group. They were a movement.

2018
Formed in Tembisa. Released debut EP 'Planting Seeds' digitally.
2019
First paid headline show. Signed to independent label. Toured SA.
2020
Released 'Green Season' during lockdown — went viral across Africa.
2021
'Paper Chase' single peaks at #1 on SA Hip-Hop charts.
2022
'Money Talks' album. 3× SAMA nominations. Arena tour across SA.
2023
Continental tour. 40+ cities. 'Gang Chronicles Vol.1' drops.
2024
'Canopy' album. International features. UK + US debut shows.
2025
'Money Tree Anthem' released. World Tour announced. Legend status.

The Vision

Where We're
Headed

"We didn't start rapping to be famous. We started to survive. Now we rap to make sure the kids behind us don't have to survive the same way we did."
— Thabo Gold (T-Gold), MTGB Founder
🌍

Global Domination

MTGB is building toward becoming the first SA hip-hop collective to headline a major US/UK festival on their own terms — no compromise, no code-switching.

🎬

Film & Visual Media

A full-length MTGB documentary and music video series are in production, chronicling the group's journey from garage to global.

🏫

Youth Music Academy

The MTGB Foundation plans to open a free music production academy in Tembisa by 2027, giving back to the community that built them.

💿

Money Tree Records

Launching their own imprint to sign and mentor emerging African artists — distribution, production, and creative control fully in their hands.

🤝

Pan-African Collabs

Deepening ties with artists from Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya and beyond to create music that unifies the continent under one sound.
